Meet Japan's Master of Horror


A brief introduction to the work of Junji Ito




  • Tomie

    Armed with the power of rebirth and a succubus-like charm, Tomie Kawakami is no ordinary girl. Driving men and women alike to insanity, she is slain over and over—only to regenerate and spread her curse... Who will be next?


  • Gyo

    The stench of death lingers over the island of Okinawa, when one day a strange, legged fish appears on the shore. So begins Tadashi and Kaori's spiral into the horror and stench of the sea... Something is definitely rotten.


  • Uzumaki

    In Kurouzu-cho, nothing is what it seems. This small, coastal town is haunted not by a person or being, but a pattern: uzumaki, the spiral. Join our unfortunate protagonists—teenagers Kirie and Shuichi—as they descend into a whirlpool of terror...





Junji Ito Collection

On January 5, 2018, an anime adaption of Ito's manga stories premiered under the name Junji Ito Collection. It ran for 12 episodes, accompanied by the release of two OVAs.
